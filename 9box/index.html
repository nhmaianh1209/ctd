Rating Definitions
Criteria for Performance and Potential classification
Performance (last 2 cycles)
High
Only HE (Highly Effective) or O (Outstanding) ratings in both of the last 2 performance cycles.
Medium
All other rating combinations — neither exclusively HE/O nor containing at least one NI.
Low
At least one NI (Needs Improvement) rating in the last 2 performance cycles.
Potential (3 indicators)
High
Demonstrates all 3: strong growth motivation + high engagement + readiness for next-level roles.
Medium
Demonstrates 1–2 indicators, or shows inconsistent potential across evaluation cycles.
Low
Does not demonstrate any of the 3 potential indicators.
