Overall Conclusion

The majority of roles score 3.5 and above, indicating that Coteccons site personnel are operating at a competent-to-proficient level. Senior positions (Project Manager, Project Director, QS Team Leader) consistently score 3.6+. Priority investment areas are execution-level engineers — particularly QS and QA/QC in MEP — with gaps in cost control, system acceptance and BIM application.
